About ACAPower

Who we are, what we believe, and how we organize.

Mission

ACAPower strengthens Asian American political engagement through education, direct action, and community care — especially on campus and in our local area.

What we do

  • Issue explanations & student-aimed civic education
  • Voter engagement and turnout efforts
  • Workshops, panels, and coalition events
  • Mutual aid + resource sharing

Values

Community-led Anti-racist Accessible Accountable Espouses Solidarity
